# What is 9Rome

9Rome is an educational school for teenagers that develops **critical thinking, argumentation, ethical analysis, and academic communication skills**.

We don't teach philosophy as abstract theory for theory's sake. We create a **practical school of thinking**, where teenagers gain skills essential for university, career, and life.

## Why Teenagers Need Philosophy

**Philosophy is not just a subject. It's a way of thinking.**

In today's world, teenagers need skills that go beyond school grades:

- **Critical thinking** ‚Äî the ability to analyze information, not just memorize it
- **Asking the right questions** ‚Äî the capacity to pose deep questions that lead to understanding
- **Logic and argumentation** ‚Äî the skill to construct logical reasoning and defend one's position
- **Ethical analysis** ‚Äî the ability to reflect on values, morality, and right decisions
- **Expressing thoughts** ‚Äî the skill to articulate ideas clearly and persuasively

These skills are critically important for UK/EU/US universities, where students must think, not just reproduce material.

## How We Teach

9Rome uses an active learning methodology:

1. **Theoretical blocks** ‚Äî classical philosophical ideas adapted for teenagers
2. **Dialogues and discussions** ‚Äî exploring real questions and problems
3. **Real-life situations** ‚Äî applying philosophical tools to life cases
4. **Debates** ‚Äî practicing argumentation and defending positions
5. **Projects** ‚Äî creating own research and presentations

**We don't memorize. We think.**

## Learning Outcomes

After completing the 9Rome program, teenagers gain:

‚úÖ **Argumentation skills** ‚Äî ability to construct logical arguments and defend viewpoints  
‚úÖ **Confidence in public speaking** ‚Äî capacity to express thoughts before an audience  
‚úÖ **Ethical thinking** ‚Äî understanding moral dilemmas and making informed decisions  
‚úÖ **Self-understanding** ‚Äî reflection on personal values and beliefs  
‚úÖ **Certificate of participation** ‚Äî official confirmation of program completion  
‚úÖ **Intellectual portfolio** ‚Äî collection of work and achievements for university applications

## 9Rome and University Philosophy

Many parents ask: "How does 9Rome differ from a university philosophy course?"

**Answer:** The depth is comparable, but the approach is adapted.

University philosophy programs (e.g., CSU Bakersfield and others) focus on the same skills: critical thinking, argument analysis, ethical reasoning, logic.

**9Rome does this earlier** ‚Äî at ages 12-18, in a more flexible and supportive format.

We prepare teenagers for university-level thinking culture **before admission**, giving them a competitive advantage.
